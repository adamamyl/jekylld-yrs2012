Important Advice

It is really important to us that your talent is not exploited and your youth is not taken advantage of. To help with this, we have produced an advice document. Please do take time to read it, you can download it from here.

Participating in SAP Young Rewired State 2012

Hopefully you've came to this page, because you're interested in YRS, and want to know more.

To take part in YRS, you need to have some knowledge of code, or spreadsheets, or some graphics skills. Don't worry if they're not too good, or if you've only been hacking for a couple of months. You'll learn more during YRS week. And have the support from others in the same boat as you — you are not alone.

You will need a laptop throughout the week. If you have a preference for dev-tools, you will want those installed in advance.

Signups for SAP Young Rewired State 2012 are now closed. If you would like to register your interest in YRS 2013, please do so here.
